Microservices

Microservices separate software systems into many small, individually deployable modules. This book shows you all you need to start with Microservices: An introduction to the architecture, the benefits and challenges as well as a practical introduction to technologies for Microservices. The book also shows the impact of Microservices on the organization and explains Nanoservices to get even smaller services. It is translated from German, see http://microservices-buch.de .
